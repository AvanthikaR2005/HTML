Top 3 Exciting Experiments

  1. Volcanic Eruption
  2. Invisible Ink
  3. Potato Power

Materials You Need

  • Volcanic Eruption: Baking Soda , Vinegar
  • Invisible Ink: Lemon Juice , Cotton Buds , White Paper
  • Potato Power: Small LED Light , Copper Wires , Zinc Nails or Coins

Instructions:


1. Volcanic Eruption: Take a small cup, add 2 spoons of baking soda. Slowly pour vinegar into it and watch the fizzing volcano erupt!
2. Invisible Ink: Dip a cotton bud in lemon juice and write on white paper. Let it dry. Gently heat the paper under a bulb or iron to reveal your secret message.
3. Potato Power: Insert one copper wire and one zinc nail into a potato. Use jumper wires to connect both ends to a small LED light. The LED will glow with your potato battery!
Always have an adult along when u do these fun Experiments!
